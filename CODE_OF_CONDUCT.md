# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all contributors, maintainers, and participants to uphold this Code of Conduct.

## Our Standards

### Be Respectful
- Treat all people with respect
- Assume good intentions
- Listen to others and accept constructive criticism

### Be Inclusive
- Welcome people of all backgrounds
- Use inclusive language
- Support underrepresented groups in tech

### Be Collaborative
- Work together toward common goals
- Share knowledge freely
- Help others succeed

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions on-topic
- Avoid harassment or discrimination
- Respect intellectual property and privacy

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Sexual or violent content
- Hate speech or slurs
- Spam or commercial exploitation
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct that negatively impacts the community

## Enforcement

### Reporting
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:
1. Contact maintainers directly: d.lovecreator@gmail.com
2. Include context and details
3. All reports are treated confidentially

### Response
We will:
1. Acknowledge your report within 48 hours
2. Investigate the incident
3. Take appropriate action (warning, suspension, removal)
4. Follow up with all parties involved

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Project events and gatherings
- Communications made in the project's name
